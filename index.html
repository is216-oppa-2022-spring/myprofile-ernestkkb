Match & Help!

With the persistence of the pandemic, working, learning and even socialisation takes on a digital presence. While this may be a convenient and a great solution to some people, there are many others who lack the appropriate tools and technologies to kick start their virtual journey. In order to solve this gap in the world, I would like to propose: Match & Help! An online platform which aims to bridge people who have products or items to donate (e.g laptop, mobile phones, stationaries) to people who are in need.
